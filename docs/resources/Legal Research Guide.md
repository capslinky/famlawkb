### Reading Case Law

**Case Structure:**
1. **Caption** - Parties and court
2. **Facts** - What happened
3. **Procedure** - How it got to court
4. **Issue** - Legal question
5. **Holding** - Court's answer
6. **Reasoning** - Why court decided
7. **Conclusion** - Final outcome
